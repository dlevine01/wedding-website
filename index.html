Blooming Hill Farm

A farm, farmstand, and restaurant that grows delicious produce and beautiful flowers. The wedding will be outside in a meadow and the reception following will be on the restaurant patio and under a tent.

How to get there

Blooming Hill Farm is located about one hour north of New York City.

The closest major airport is Newark.

There is limited New Jersey Transit access to neighboring towns. There is bus service to Warwick and train service to Harriman.

The easiest way to get there and around the area is by car. If want to carpool with friends or would like help arranging a ride, please let us know and we'd be happy to assist!

For taxis, our venue has recommendedAll County Car Service.

Where to stay

 

We haven't booked a hotel block. There are lots of rental houses, hotels, and bed & breakfasts in the area, including the ones listed below.

Warwick, about 25 minutes away from Blooming Hill Farm, is the largest and most active town in the area. It's definitely the region's hub, with a few diners and coffee shops, bars, and stores to meander through. Montgomery is also a nice little town, with some cute spots to grab a bite.
